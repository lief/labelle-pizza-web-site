Opening hours and delivery

We open at 5pm on Thursdays, Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays; when you can come and buy any of our wide range of takeaway pizzas. If you want to order a pizza to be delivered, please call us and we'll arrange for a local taxi service to deliver your pizza. Please note that delivery by taxi costs extra: the price of the taxi ride per usual rates.
